How to use SeekSpeak:

  1. Open any YouTube video with captions
  2. Press Ctrl+Shift+F or click Search
  3. Type to search captions instantly
  4. Click results to jump to that moment

Troubleshooting:

If the extension isn't loading:

  • Refresh the YouTube page
  • Make sure you're on a video page (not the homepage)
  • Check that the video has captions available
